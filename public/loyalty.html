Square POS Integration

Auto-Apply Rewards at POS

When a customer earns a reward, a Customer Group Discount is automatically created in Square. This discount will auto-apply at checkout when the customer is identified at the POS.

How it works:
  1. Customer buys qualifying items and earns a reward in your system
  2. A 100% discount is automatically created in Square, limited to this customer
  3. When customer returns and is identified at POS (phone lookup), the free item discount auto-applies
  4. After they redeem, the discount is automatically cleaned up
